H.O.P.E. Programs
(Helping Other People Excel)

Our Programs are designed around H.O.P.E., which stands for "Helping Other People Excel." We have some AMAZING programs that make a difference “Across the Street” and “Around the World!" Our H.O.P.E. Programs provide help, both spiritually and physically, to those in need, such as the hungry, the homeless, the orphans, as well as those impacted by disasters; locally, nationally, and abroad. We don’t just send supplies, we are the “hands and feet of Jesus” on the ground to directly impact the lives of those in need. You too can be the “hands and feet of Jesus” by participating in one of our programs. Check our "Home" page or Facebook for latest opportunities.

Jesus said in Matthew 25:37-40, “Then the righteous will answer Him, saying, ‘Lord, when did we see You hungry and feed You, or thirsty and give You drink? When did we see You a stranger and take You in, or naked and clothe You? Or when did we see You sick, or in prison, and come to You?’ And the King will answer and say to them, ‘Assuredly, I say to you, inasmuch as you did it to one of the least of these My brethren, you did it to Me.’
